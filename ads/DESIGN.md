# 90 PROJECT Video Ad Visual System

## Style Prompt

Premium black-gold catering brand with warm restaurant lighting, cinematic food atmosphere, confident Chinese-first typography, and a clean WhatsApp lead-generation focus. The ad should feel elegant, trustworthy, practical, and hungry-making rather than loud or cluttered.

## Colors

- Black canvas: `#0e0b08`
- Deep coffee black: `#17120d`
- Gold: `#d6a85c`
- Bright gold: `#ffdf9a`
- Cream text: `#fff7e8`
- Warm muted copy: `#ead7b8`

## Typography

- Chinese: `Microsoft YaHei`, `Noto Sans SC`, sans-serif fallback.
- English and numbers: `Arial`, `Helvetica`, sans-serif fallback.
- Headlines use heavy weight, large size, and tight but readable spacing.
- Data such as `RM300`, `20 餐`, and phone numbers use tabular numbers.

## Motion

- Use premium focus-pull, blur crossfade, slow warm light movement, and upward text reveal.
- Keep scene pacing calm but decisive: 15 seconds total, 5 clear beats.
- Do not over-animate. The offer and WhatsApp CTA must stay readable.

## What Not To Do

- Do not use purple/blue neon, cute stickers, or busy decorative blobs.
- Do not make alcohol/cocktail the main message.
- Do not mention packaging showcase in this ad.
- Do not overcrowd the first frame; one hook, one offer, one CTA.
- Do not promise fixed delivery coverage or guaranteed pricing beyond the listed package examples.
